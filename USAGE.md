# USAGE.md

## Recommended Business Contexts

### `bar_chart/`

- Quarterly performance reviews
- Business unit comparisons
- Investor and board summary decks

### `pie_chart/`

- Portfolio or mix breakdowns
- Revenue composition summaries
- Allocation overviews

### `progress_bar/`

- Program status
- Rollout completion
- Delivery checkpoint updates

### `stat_card/`

- KPI highlights
- Executive snapshots
- Earnings and operations summaries

### `sparkline_card/`

- Trend callouts
- Lightweight telemetry summaries
- Weekly or monthly activity reads

### `bullet_chart/`

- Actual-versus-target performance
- Capacity or coverage tracking
- Goal attainment communication

### `timeline_steps/`

- Release plans
- Program phases
- Transformation milestone tracking

### `leaderboard_card/`

- Performance ranking
- Team scorecards
- Campaign or sales standings

## Recommended Pairings

- `stat_card` + `sparkline_card` for KPI plus trend
- `bar_chart` + `bullet_chart` for mix plus target attainment
- `progress_bar` + `timeline_steps` for status plus phase context

## Composition Recommendations

### `executive_dashboard.html`

### `investor_update.html`
