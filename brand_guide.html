Principles

Reduce Typography and spacing over decoration. Small number of surfaces and emphasis states.
Signal, not atmosphere The accent color marks deliberate emphasis. It doesn't fill backgrounds.
Static reliability Every page opens directly in a browser. No build step, no framework.
Accessible by default Screen-reader summaries on data components. Visible focus states. Reduced-motion support.
